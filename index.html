Khon Kaen (formerly Kham Kaen) is the 6th largest province in the northeastern region by area. and has the third largest population in the Northeast. It is in the group of central northeastern provinces, namely Roi Et, Khon Kaen, Maha Sarakham, Kalasin, with Khon Kaen being the province that is the group's operations center.
